FISH EAT SOUND EFFECTS
=======================
Place your fish-eating sound files here.

Expected file:
  - fishEat.wav   (or fishEat.mp3 / fishEat.ogg)

This plays when the fish enemy eats food dropped into the ocean.
Triggered by the fish:ateFood event emitted from the BoarSystem.
Suggested: a gulp, chomp, or bubble splash sound.
